More Children

Convenience functions to work with children: 
(defun list-children (node) ...) => fresh list

(defmacro do-children ((var node &optional result) &body body) ...)

Also: nth-child, first-child, last-child, previous-sibling, next-sibling

In addition, lots of sequence-like functions are provided: 
(defun find-child (value parent &key from-end start end key test) ...)
  • find-child[-if]
  • count-children[-if]
  • child-position[-if]
  • filter-children
